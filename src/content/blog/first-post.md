Amazon S3 is one of the most widely used storage services in AWS, but it's also one of the most commonly misconfigured services. Here's how to keep your data safe.

## 1. Block Public Access by Default

Always enable "Block Public Access" at the account level. This prevents accidental exposure of sensitive data.

## 2. Use Bucket Policies Wisely

Bucket policies are powerful but can be complex. Follow the principle of least privilege.

## 3. Enable Encryption

Use server-side encryption (SSE-S3 or SSE-KMS) for all buckets.
